Our Story

Community, Country
& Good Food

92 Golf Kitchen & Canteen is a veteran-owned and operated food truck proudly serving the Lake Worth and Greenacres community. Founded by Nick & Dianne, the canteen was built on a simple idea: exceptional food, fair prices, and a genuine love for the people they serve.

You'll find us parked near Copper Point Brewery and right at home at 6830 Long Key Street. Whether you're grabbing a hearty breakfast before a round of golf, a quick lunch, or a satisfying dinner, we bring the same patriotic spirit and hospitality to every plate.

We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients because your community deserves nothing less. Welcome to the canteen — pull up a chair, dogs welcome.
